Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Angelo Games Studio collects, uses, stores and protects information when you visit this website, contact us, or interact with our games and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, topic selection and message content when you use the contact form or contact us by email.

2. Usage Data

We may collect limited technical data such as browser type, device information, pages visited, approximate location, referring pages and timestamps for security, analytics and website improvement.

3. Cookies and Analytics

If analytics or cookie-based tools are enabled, they may help us understand how visitors use the website. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. How We Use Information

  • To respond to your messages and requests.
  • To improve our website, games and services.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations where required.

5. Legal Basis

Where applicable under GDPR or UK GDPR, processing may be based on your consent, our legitimate interests, performance of a requested service, or legal obligations.

6. Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information. However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards to protect transferred information.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us so we can review and delete it where required.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, including the EU, UK, California, Canada, South Korea and Australia, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, request portability, withdraw consent, or lodge a complaint with a regulator.

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents may have rights under CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know, delete, correct, opt out of certain sharing, and not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights. We do not sell personal information.

12. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for hosting, contact forms, analytics, email delivery or game distribution. These providers process information according to their own privacy practices.

13. Links to Other Websites

Our website may link to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

15. Contact
